CREATOR, WRITER, AUTHOR

American writer, TV screenwriter. She began her writing career in 1991 as a creator of television programs for children. She also created scripts for Nickelodeon stations, including: “Klarysa will explain everything”, “Little Bear” and “Oswald”. She was also the main creator of the story of “Clifford's dog's” early years on the Minimini Channel. She received a Writers Guild of America nomination for co-writing the story “Christmas Special, santa, Baby!”.
